Docker Control Center backgrounds

Accepted filenames for each theme:
- Light: theme_light.jpg or light.png
- Dark: theme_dark.jpg or dark.png
- Black: theme_black.jpg or black.png

Supported formats:
- .jpg
- .jpeg
- .png
- .webp

Recommended resolution:
- 3840x2160 or larger
- wide composition with calm space for UI overlays

The app uses cover scaling:
- image fills the entire app background
- image is cropped from the sides if needed
- image is never stretched out of aspect ratio
